Privacy Policy for Pizzeria

Effective Date: June 6,2023 - June 6,2024

1. Introduction At Pizzeria

We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use and protect that information, and the choices you have regarding your information when using our online pizza ordering service. By using our website, you agree to the terms and practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information: When you create an account or place an order, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and delivery address. This information is necessary to process and fulfill your orders and provide you with the requested services.
b. Payment Information: To process your online orders, we collect payment information, including credit card details, billing address, and other relevant payment details. We use third-party payment processors that adhere to industry-standard security measures to handle and process payment transactions securely.
c. Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access our website, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers. This information helps us analyze trends, understand user preferences, and improve the functionality and performance of our website.
d. Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect information about how you use our website. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences, track your activities on our website, and provide personalized content and advertisements. You have the option to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings.
e. Log Files: Our servers automatically record certain information when you access or use our website. This information may include your IP address, browser type, referring/exit pages, and timestamps. We use this information to analyze trends, administer the website, track user movements, and gather demographic information for aggregate use.
f. User Generated Content: If you choose to submit reviews, comments, or other user-generated content on our website, we may collect and store that information. Please note that any information you disclose in these public areas becomes publicly available and may be collected and used by others.

3. Use of Information

a. Order Processing: We use the collected information to process and fulfill your pizza orders, communicate with you about your order status, and provide customer support. This includes verifying payment details, delivering the order to the correct address, and contacting you for order-related notifications or updates.
b. Personalization: We may use your information to personalize your experience on our website, including remembering your preferences, suggesting relevant offers or promotions, and displaying customized content or advertisements.
c. Marketing Communications: With your consent, we may send you promotional emails or SMS messages about special offers, new products, or updates. You can opt-out of receiving these communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions included in our emails or by contacting us directly.
d. Analytics and Improvements: We analyze user data to understand trends, monitor the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns, improve our website, and enhance our services. This may include analyzing user interactions, demographics, and preferences to optimize our website's performance and user experience.
e. Legal Compliance: We may use or disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or government request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others. This may include sharing information with law enforcement authorities or other third parties in response to a subpoena, court order, or similar legal procedure.

4. Data Sharing

a. Service Providers: We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, delivering orders, and conducting business activities on our behalf. These service providers are contractually obligated to handle your information securely and only for the purposes specified by us.
b. Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you through prominent notice on our website and seek your consent if your information is to be used or disclosed in a manner different from this Privacy Policy.
c. Aggregated or Anonymized Data: We may share aggregated or anonymized data that does not personally identify you for marketing, advertising, research, or other purposes. This information may be used to generate statistics, insights, or industry reports.

5. Data Security

a. We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We implement technical, physical, and administrative safeguards to secure the information we collect and store.
b. We use secure socket layer technology (SSL) to encrypt data transmitted between your device and our servers during the ordering and payment process. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
c. We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. When we no longer need your information, we will securely dispose of it in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

6. Your Choices

a. Account Settings: You can review and update your account information by logging into your account on our website. It is your responsibility to keep your account information accurate and up to date.
b. Communication Preferences: You can choose to opt-out of receiving marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions included in our emails or by contacting us directly. However, please note that you may still receive transactional or service-related communications, such as order confirmations or updates.
c. Cookies: Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. You have the option to modify your browser settings to block or delete cookies if you prefer. However, please note that blocking or deleting cookies may affect certain features or functionality of our website.

7. Children's Privacy

a. Our website is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us, and we will take immediate steps to delete the information.

8. Third-Party Links

a. Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. These third-party websites have their privacy policies, which we encourage you to review. We do not assume any responsibility for the content, privacy practices, or actions of these third-party websites or services.

9. International Data Transfers

a. If you access or use our website outside of the country where your personal information is collected, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries with different privacy laws. By using our website, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence.

10. Changes to the Privacy Policy

a. We reserve the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. The updated Privacy Policy will be posted on our website with the revised effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at pizzeria69@gmail.com. We will address your inquiries and strive to resolve any privacy-related issues promptly and in accordance with applicable laws.
